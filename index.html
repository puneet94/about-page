ABOUT

YallaSpree was founded by a bunch of BITS graduates in 2012, with an aim to use technology to make shopping more fun and engaging.

Amongst our team are scuba drivers, origami artists, certified para-gliders and a bunch of young people with a never-say-die attitude.
